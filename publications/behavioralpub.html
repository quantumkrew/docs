MAYSI-2 Training Video

Tom Grisso, Ph.D. & Richard Barnum, M.D.

We have developed a MAYSI-2 Training Video. One of the authors of the MAYSI-2, Thomas Grisso, Ph.D., speaks over automated Power Points and guides users through the MAYSI-2 training.

This software is free to all registered MAYSI-2 users. If your site has purchased the 2006 MAYSI-2 manual and has submitted the MAYSI-2 form to NYSAP, then you are qualified to receive the free training video.

This video is available for download and it will download quickly if you have a high speed internet connection. To request a copy of the MAYSI-2 Training Video, please complete the Request Form. You will receive a response within 1 business day.
